About me

Hi there. My name is Ian, and I'm a researcher based in Tbilisi, Georgia. I've spent most of the last fifteen years working for international non-profits, largely in the South Caucasus and the Middle East. You can find my full professional profile on LinkedIn and my code at GitHub.

I enjoy mucking about with computers, particularly building things and exploring data with Python. My research interests include aid, inequality, media, and drug policy.

About this site

This blog exists primarily to document and support my learning. By writing about what I do, I understand things better, and have more motivation to complete what I'm working on before moving to the next shiny thing.

It's built using Jekyll, using the Clean Blog Jekyll theme by Start Bootstrap.
