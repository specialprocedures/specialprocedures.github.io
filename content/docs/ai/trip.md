# Stable Diffusion 1.5

I grabbed a bunch of quotes from science fiction authors, and dumped them as prompts into Stable Diffusion 1.5.

## William Gibson
> *Cyberspace. A consensual hallucination experienced daily by billions of legitimate operators, in every nation, by children being taught mathematical concepts... A graphic representation of data abstracted from banks of every computer in the human system. Unthinkable complexity. Lines of light ranged in the nonspace of the mind, clusters and constellations of data. Like city lights, receding...*

## Rudy Rucker

For me, the best thing about Cyberpunk is that it taught me how to enjoy shopping malls, which used to terrify me. Now I just imagine the whole thing is two miles below the moon’s surface, and that half the people’s right-brains have been eaten by roboticized steel rats. And suddenly it’s interesting again.

{{< grid dir="images/ai/0afd4f48ca024578611a0e2468b8753c" columns="2" >}}

The silence is your answer.

## Neal Stephenson
He turns off the techno-shit in his goggles.<br>All it does is confuse him.

He stands there reading statistics<br>about his own death<br>even as it's happening to him.

Very post-modern.

## Blade Runner
I've seen things...<br>seen things you little people wouldn't believe.

Attack ships on fire off the shoulder of Orion bright as magnesium...<br>I rode on the back decks of a blinker<br>and watched C-beams glitter in the dark<br>near the Tannhäuser Gate.

All those moments...<br>they'll be gone.
